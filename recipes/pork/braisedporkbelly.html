PORK: BRAISED PORK BELLY


Suspiciously easy for what you end up with. Recipe is for dinner for 2.

  • 8oz pork belly, cut into 1" cubes.
  • 1 (or 2 small) green onion, cut into 2" pieces
  • 2 slices ginger, smashed
  • 2 cloves of garlic, peeled
  • 1/4 cup shaoxing wine
  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp sugar

Put cubed pork belly in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a boil, skimming foam from the water as it appears until water is mostly clear (3ish minutes.)

Remove pork belly from pot; clean pot and return pork belly to it, along with green onion, garlic and ginger. Add 1/4 cup shaoxing wine and enough water to just cover the pork. Add light and dark soy sauce and sugar.

Bring to a boil and reduce temperature to a simmer. Simmer, covered, 60-90 minutes, until pork is tender. More water can be added if necessary to continue cooking if it boils off too fast.
