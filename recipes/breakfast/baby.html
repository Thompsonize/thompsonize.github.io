Breakfast: Dutch Baby


Angela makes these for breakfast sometimes and the effort to deliciousness ratio is high.

  • 3/4 cup ap flour
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3 egg
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • slightly less than 3 tbsp butter (for the pan)

    For serving: butter, jelly, syrup, fruit, cooked apples and cinnamon, powdered sugar, etc.

Put all ingredients excepting the butter and toppings in a blender. Blend 10 seconds, scrape down the sides, and blend another 10 seconds. (Angela uses a stick blender and a big cup).

Let the batter rest 20ish minutes to hydrate the flour and pre-heat a cast iron pan in a 400F oven.

After the 20 minutes are up, melt the butter in the hot pan and tilt the pan around so it's coated with butter.

Blend the batter one last time and pour it into the hot pan.

Bake until the pancake is puffed up and golden brown with crispy, darker brown edges, approx 20 minutes

Remove from oven. Cut into slices with kitchen shears. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and eat with fruit or jam, syrup, etc.

1 pancake made this way stuffs 2 people.
