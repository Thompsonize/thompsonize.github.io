Chicken: Tacos


This hits the spot (better) that the taco bell not delivering to you leaves.

Trim fat from boneless skinless chicken thighs.

Create a marinade from adobo, salt, chicken tomato powder, sugar, black pepper, freshly toasted and ground cumin seed, a chopped chile canned in adobo, the juice of a lime (or half an acidic orange, or some pickled jalapeno etc juice, or white, wine, or apple vinegar, or a combination) and olive oil.

Dredge chicken thighs throughly in marinade and layer in a glass bowl or measuring cup or similar container with a sturdy bottom. Top with remaining liquid, and pierce the stacked chicken thighs repeatedly with (metal or plastic, not wood or disposable) chopsticks, or skewers.

Marinate at room temperature an hour (or in the fridge for longer)

Preheat broiler. Arrange chicken in 1 later on an oven rack over a pan. Broil 3"-4" from top element until edges are crispy, 10ish minutes, then flip to finish (5ish minutes). Crispier is better. Cool and slice.

Warm corn tortillas in a warm dry pan and keep in a folded towel. Assemble and top as desired.
